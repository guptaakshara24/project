Must-Visit Places in Ladakh

~Explore the stunning landscapes, high-altitude wonders, and cultural treasures of Ladakh.

Pangong Lake

A breathtaking high-altitude lake famous for its crystal-clear waters that change color throughout the day. Pangong Lake stretches across India and China and offers unforgettable scenic beauty.

Khardung La Pass

One of the highest motorable roads in the world, Khardung La offers thrilling rides and panoramic views. It serves as the gateway to Nubra Valley and is a dream destination for bikers.

Monasteries

Ladakh is home to ancient Buddhist monasteries like Thiksey and Hemis. These monasteries showcase beautiful architecture, spiritual traditions, and vibrant festivals.

Leh Palace

A historic royal palace overlooking Leh town, offering insight into Ladakh’s royal heritage. From the top, visitors can enjoy stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Nubra Valley

Known for its cold desert landscapes, sand dunes, and double-humped camels. Nubra Valley offers a unique blend of natural beauty and peaceful village life.

Zanskar Valley

A remote and rugged valley famous for trekking, rafting, and dramatic mountain scenery. Zanskar is perfect for adventure lovers seeking untouched natural beauty.

Tso Moriri Lake

A serene high-altitude lake located in a protected area. It is home to migratory birds and offers a peaceful escape surrounded by mountains.

Shanti Stupa

A beautiful white-domed Buddhist stupa built to promote peace. It offers stunning sunrise and sunset views over Leh town.

Hemis National Park

India’s largest national park and a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. It is famous for snow leopards, Himalayan blue sheep, and rare bird species.
